About Us

Hindustan Aquatech

About Us

Hindustan Aquatech is a professional water treatment company formed by a team of water analysts and technical water experts having a combined experience of over 25 years.

The company has thorough expertise in water treatment and offers a wide range of products and solutions catering to residential, industrial and commercial segments.

We have a team of professionals with deep expertise in biotechnology, chemical engineering, wastewater design and advanced water engineering solutions.

We offer all types of water and wastewater management solutions to meet the needs of customers of all sizes and industries.

Our Vision

To be a global leader in water treatment and wastewater management, recognized for our cutting-edge solutions, environmental stewardship, and dedication to creating a cleaner, healthier, and more sustainable future for all.

Our Mission

To deliver innovative, reliable, and sustainable water and wastewater management solutions that meet the needs of residential, industrial, and commercial clients. We are committed to improving water quality, conserving resources, and providing exceptional customer service through advanced technology and professional expertise.

Why Choose Us

  • Customized RO water purifiers and fully automatic softener systems.
  • Electromagnetic water conditioners and SS 304 water dispensers.
  • Ultraviolet (UV) treatment systems and ultra filters (UF).
  • Nano filters, silver nano technology, and alkaline filters.
  • Rain harvesting systems and ozone purifiers.
  • CDI & DM plants, ETP & STP solutions.
